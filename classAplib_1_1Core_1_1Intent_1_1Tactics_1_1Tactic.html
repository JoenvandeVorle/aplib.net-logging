Detailed Description

Tactics are the real meat of Desire.Goals.Goals, as they define how the agent can approach the goal in hopes of finding a solution which makes the Goal's heuristic function evaluate to being completed. A tactic represents a smart combination of Actions, which are executed in a Believe Desire Intent Cycle.
